<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
<strong>面向未来的赶考</strong></p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
<strong>　　——习近平总书记指导河北省党的群众路线教育实践活动回访记</strong></p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　新华社北京3月23日电(新华社记者李从军、赵承、李柯勇)65年前的今天——1949年3月23日，西柏坡，一个特殊的历史节点。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　土坯房前，老槐树下，握别依依不舍的父老乡亲，毛泽东率领中共中央机关动身前往北平。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　春风料峭，朝阳流金。毛泽东面带微笑，大手一挥：“进京赶考去！”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2013年7月11日，同是西柏坡，同是一个重要的历史节点。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　站在九月会议旧址前，习近平神色凝重，目光穿越历史。此时中国，正处在全面建成小康社会、实现中华民族伟大复兴中国梦的关键阶段。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　面对历史和未来，他再次提出“赶考”问题：“要继续把人民对我们党的‘考试’、把我们党正在经受和将要经受各种考验的‘考试’考好，努力交出优异的答卷！”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　赶考在继续。在新的历史起点上，以习近平同志为总书记的党中央发起了群众路线教育实践活动。习近平将西柏坡所在的河北省作为第一批教育实践活动联系点，深入实际，指导工作，推动全党教育实践活动向纵深拓展。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　与此同时，其他中央政治局常委李克强、张德江、俞正声、刘云山、王岐山、张高丽分别到广西、江苏、甘肃、浙江、黑龙江、四川指导教育实践活动。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　这是一次跨越历史、面向未来的“赶考”。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　一场心灵洗礼的行动，在中华大地迅速深入展开。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　最近，记者深入河北，回访习近平总书记指导河北省开展群众路线教育实践活动的过程，感受这一活动给燕赵大地带来的巨大变化，感受我们党在新时期赶考路上正在书写的壮美答卷。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　<strong>考题之一：“总开关”拧得紧不紧</strong></p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　坚定理想信念，切实解决好世界观、人生观、价值观这个“总开关”问题。理想信念就是共产党人精神上的“钙”，没有理想信念，理想信念不坚定，精神上就会“缺钙”，就会得“软骨病”。“总开关”问题没有解决好，这样那样的出轨越界、跑冒滴漏就在所难免。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　——习近平</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　有了理想，才有活的灵魂。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　丧失信念，堤坝就会崩溃。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平将共产党人的信仰，形象地称为“总开关”。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　赶考路上，教育实践活动中，这成了第一大考题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　面对各种诱惑和挑战，习近平号召全体共产党员，“坚守崇高信仰，炼就金刚不坏之身”。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　燕赵大地，自古多慷慨悲歌之士。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　现代革命史上，这片土地哺育的一代又一代共产党人血沃中华。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　1927年春，寒气袭人。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　森然兀立的绞刑架下，李大钊目光坦荡，平静如澈“不能因为你们今天绞死了我，就绞死了伟大的共产主义！”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　一个共产主义者倒下了，千万个后继者奋起，光照神州。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平指出：“在我们党90多年的历史中，一代又一代共产党人为了追求民族独立和人民解放，不惜流血牺牲，的就是一种信仰，为的就是一个理想。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　正是因为信仰，革命时期，共产党人高唱“砍头不要紧，只要主义真”，前赴后继；</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　正是因为信仰，和平年代，共产党人“心中装着人民，唯独没有自己”，鞠躬尽瘁；</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　正是因为信仰，共产党人引领中华民族，从百年沉沦走向民族复兴。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平说：“我们党现在已经90多岁，马上要进入百年了。一个政党，如一个人一样，最宝贵的是历尽沧涩还怀有一颗赤子之心。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　理想信念的缺失，乃百病之源。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平指出，如果丢失了共产党人的远大目标，就会迷失方向，变成功利主义、实用主义者，最后意志消沉，奉行及时行乐的人生哲学，甚至产生“人不为己，天诛地灭”的想法，把当干部作为一种谋取私利、巧取豪夺的手段。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“总开关”一旦松动，行为、作风焉能无恙</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平在河北省委常委班子专题民主生活会上说，有一个道理要反复讲，就是党的干部必须永不动摇信仰，矢志不渝为中国特色社会主义共同理想而奋斗。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2013年9月23日下午，石家庄，民主生活会正在进行中。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“坚定理想信念，应该从我们走过的道路上去体会它、认识它……”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　插话的，是习近平。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　党的最高领导人全程参加一个省委的民主生活会，史无前例。这个会究竟怎么才能开得好这给河北省委领导班子出了一道难度很大的考题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2013年8月，习近平对前来汇报的河北省领导开门见山：“我参加你们的专题民主生活会，可不是听你们讲莺歌燕舞的，要有真正的批评和自我批评。”他告诫说：“现在民主生活会，往往是对上级放‘礼炮’，对同级放‘哑炮’，对自己放‘空炮’，最后是你好、我好、大家都好。这样无助于解决问题。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　总书记的指导，如春风沐面，又如泰山压顶。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　河北动了真格，省委常委们反思自己“走过的道路”，不遮掩、不回避，敢于揭短亮丑，批评和自我批评的锋芒直指世界观、人生观、价值观的根本问题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　河北省委书记周本顺代表班子集体检讨，剖析“四风”产生的原因，总病根就是理想信念淡薄了，“补钙”强骨抓得不够经郴总源头就是理论学习放松了，把中国特色社会主义理论体系内化于心、外化于行做得不够。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　志在顶峰者，不会半坡退却。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　信念迷失者，常有歧路彷徨。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“反思自己，心理不平衡，自我膨胀，这几年开拓进取的精神松懈、干事创业的劲头不足，从根本上来说是理想信念不够坚定，是精神上的‘缺钙’。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“怕接触矛盾，多栽花少栽刺，当‘圆滑官’，表面看是一个处世哲学问题，根子还是理想信念蒙上了灰尘，思想路线出现了模糊。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　……</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　一种久违的率真会风，让众人凛然振作。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　前后4个半天的民主生活会，既有红红脸、出出汗的紧张和严肃，又有加加油、鼓鼓劲的宽松与和谐。在尖锐与坦诚中，大家不断加深着对理想信念的认识。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　点评时，习近平再次突出强调“总开关”——</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“你们都知道温水煮青蛙的故事吧”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　他接着说，温水煮，青蛙不知不觉就死去了。一个人也是如此，职务升迁了，生活变好了，信念就可能慢慢消失了。如果你自己不去自我警醒，我们作为一个党不能去自我警醒，那么谁还能叫醒你呢</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　说者从容论析，连类感发；听者醍醐灌顶，心潮澎湃。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　新华社、中央电视台记者深入采访，集中反映这场民主生活会的长篇纪实、焦点访谈迅即播发。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　举国震动！中央和国家机关、各省区市领导班子连夜组织收看，以此为范例，纷纷召开高质量的专题民主生活会。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　俗话说，大凡一户人家馒头蒸得好，只需把他的面团给别人，全村的馒头便都会发得好。这，就是酵母的作用。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“照镜子、正衣冠、洗洗澡、治治病。”教育实践活动，让很多党员、干部经历了一条从不自觉到警醒、由不情愿到震动的心路历程，经受了一次先“痛”后“快”的心灵涤荡。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在学习思考和查摆剖析中，党员、干部正视理想动摇、信仰迷失、精神“缺钙”、道德失范问题，清洗思想灰尘，清除政治微生物，为世界观、人生观、价值观这个“总开关”拧紧了螺丝。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　大海航行，唯有信仰的灯塔才能穿透迷雾，指引前行的方向。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　毛泽东、邓小平、江泽民、胡锦涛，都对此作过深刻论述。他们是坚守理想信念的典范，无论遇到何等风浪，都矢志不移。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　1925年，在填写“少年中国学会”改组委员会征询意见调查表时，毛泽东写道：“本人信仰共产主义，主张无产阶级的社会革命。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　1985年，邓小平在中国共产党全国代表会议上指出：“过去我们党无论怎样弱小，无论遇到什么困难，一直有强大的战斗力，因为我们有马克思主义和共产主义的信念。有了共同的理想，也就有了铁的纪律。无论过去、现在和将来，这都是我们的真正优势。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　1999年，江泽民在纪念中国共产党成立七十八周年座谈会上强调：“我们共产党人的根本政治信仰是社会主义和共产主义，世界观是马克思主义的辩证唯物主义和历史唯物主义，这是任何时候都丝毫不能动摇的。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2006年，胡锦涛在纪念红军长征胜利70周年大会上指出：“崇高理想，坚定信念，是凝聚人心、催人奋进的伟大旗帜，是战胜困难、赢得胜利的力量源泉。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2013年7月，教育实践活动启动后，曾多次到过西柏坡的习近平，重回西柏坡，重温“进京赶考”，重新沐浴信仰之光。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　他说，我怀着崇敬之心来、带着许多思考走。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　这一次，他思考些什么呢</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　也许，他在思考，在机遇与挑战并存的历史交汇点上，在风云变幻的世界舞台上，如何把握中国的前途和命运；</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　也许，他在思考，实现“两个一百年”奋斗目标，还需要付出哪些艰辛努力；</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　也许，他思考的仅仅是怎样着眼当下，把教育实践活动扎实有效地推向深入……</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　无论如何，他思考的核心都离不开这一点——共产党人怎样更好地承担历史和人民赋予的责任。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　<strong>考题之二：作风“篱笆”扎得严不严</strong></p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　作风问题关系人心向背，关系党的执政基础。我们一定要牢记“奢靡之始，危亡之渐”的古训，对作风之弊、行为之垢来一次大排查、大检修、大扫除，切实解决人民群众反映强烈的突出问题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　——习近平</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　1983年夏，河北正定县农村，一辆吉普车陷在泥泞里。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　有干部见村民走了过来，便请帮着推车。不料，见是当官的，村民不仅不推，嘴上还骂骂咧咧。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　那位干部正要发火，一旁的新任县委书记习近平一把拉住他，说：“群众为什么骂人应该反思我们自己。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　不久，正定县改进领导作风六项规定出台。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　正定老干部程宝怀说，今天的中央八项规定与当年正定六项规定体现了同样的革命传统。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　翻开30年前那份文件，作风俨然成为关键词。“反对官衙作风，注重工作实效”、“以身作则，不搞不正之风”，还有限制会议、接待等活动的要求，历历在目。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　令人感慨的是，上面赫然写道：“对各种不正之风，要坚持原则，敢问、敢顶、敢管，敢于碰硬。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　更令人感慨的是，30年时光飞逝，当年那种勇毅依然萦驻于怀，呼之欲出——</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　如今，当共产党人踏上赶考的新征程，重点审视的还是作风。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　先是中央八项规定出台。随之，教育实践活动聚焦形式主义、官僚主义、享乐主义和奢靡之风这“四风”问题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　作风，看似无形，却无处不在。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　作风，看似平超又与群众息息相关。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　用“延安作风”打败蒋介石的“西安作风”——毛泽东这句话，道出了共产党人夺取政权的奥秘：以优良作风赢得人心、赢得天下。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　成也作风，败也作风。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平惟妙惟肖地给“四风”画了四幅“肖像”：</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　形式主义——知行不一、不求实效，文山会海、花拳绣腿，贪图虚名、弄虚作假。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　官僚主义——脱离实际、脱离群众，高高在上、漠视现实，唯我独尊、自我膨胀。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　享乐主义——精神懈怠、不思进取，追名逐利、贪图享受，讲究排场、玩风盛行。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　奢靡之风——铺张浪费、挥霍无度，大兴土木、节庆泛滥，生活奢华、骄奢淫逸，甚至以权谋私、腐化堕落。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　河北的一些领导干部说，对照一下，就会出一身冷汗。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　俗话说，“篱笆扎得紧，野狗野猫钻不进”。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　作风“篱笆”，该如何扎严扎紧呢</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　善禁者，先禁其身而后人。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2013年7月，习近平在与河北省委领导班子成员座谈时说：“邓小平同志说过：‘在中国来说，谁有资格犯大错误就是中国共产党。’那么在党内，谁有资格犯大错误我看还是高级干部。高级干部一旦犯错误，造成的危害大，对党的形象和威信损害大。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　这道“篱笆”，首先从中央政治局“扎”起。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　自中央八项规定出台以来，中央政治局认真贯彻落实，在改进调查研究、精简会议活动、精简文件简报、规范出访活动、改进警卫工作、改进新闻报道、严格文稿发表、厉行勤俭节约等方面，为全党加强作风建设带了一个好头。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平身体力行：先后十几次国内考察，不封路、不清场、不扰民。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平对河北省委常委们说，作风面前人人平等，你们要以普通党员身份把自己摆进去，马克思主义手电筒既照别人更照自己。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　河北省委常委班子专题民主生活会上，有人谈到越野车配备问题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平插话：“我看常委们不要配越野车，书记、省长也不要配。真要下乡，就临时调用。”他还说，中央下一步要就此作出规定。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　很快，河北省委常委们的越野车全部上交。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　清理超标办公用番从书记、省长开始。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　清理“吃空饷”，纪委监察厅先清出4个人。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　……</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　但立直标，终无曲影。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　教育实践活动开展半年多来，干部队伍风气为之一新。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　各地区各部门纷纷大幅精简会议、文件、考核评比；行政审批普遍提速三成以上；“三公”经费普遍压缩，节庆论坛展会、公费出国团组更是减少了半数以上……</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　老百姓拍手说，这回可是来真的了！</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　扎作风“篱笆”，须防微杜渐——</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2013年9月3日，“严禁用公款送月饼送节礼”，中央一声令下，商场里高档月饼出现从未有过的滞销。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　以中央名义管公款月饼，细微之举，显见态度之真、决心之大！</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　20天后，习近平在河北省委常委班子专题民主生活会上说，那就是抓月饼里面隐藏的腐败嘛。虽然看起来好像事情不大，但针尖大的窟窿能透过斗大的风，一定要防微杜渐、一尘不染、两袖清风。抓了中秋节抓国庆节，抓了国庆节抓新年，抓了新年抓春节，抓了春节抓清明节、抓端午节，就这么抓下去，总会见效的，使之形成一种习惯、一种风气。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　抓滥用公款、抓借机敛财、抓冒吃空饷……2013年，河北在全省开展正风肃纪专项行动，共查处违反中央八项规定精神的问题1723件，处分2445人。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　截至2013年底，全国共查处违反中央八项规定精神的问题24521起，处理30420人，给予党纪政纪处分7692人。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平说：“办好一件事后再办第二件事，让大家感到我们是能办成事的，而且是认真办事的。这样才能取信于民、取信于全党。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　扎作风“篱笆”，要扎严制度的笼子——</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在作风问题上，并非没有“篱笆”，但“四风”乃至腐败行为却屡禁不止，为什么</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平打了个生动的比方：“牛栏关猫是关不住的，空隙太大，猫可以来去自如。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在教育实践活动中，建章立制，扎紧约束权力的“笼子”。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在党员干部直接联系群众、畅通群众诉求反映渠道、厉行节约反对浪费、民主决策和工作落实、干部选拔任用和考核评价等方面，一批党内法规得以建立健全。“红线”划出来了，“高压线”架起来了。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　每个人都感受到了身边的变化。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　西柏坡，记者与当地群众座谈。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在景区开餐馆的陈素梅说：“公款买单、点大菜的人少了，自掏腰包、点家常菜的散客多了。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　关于作风考题，65年前，革命先辈曾留下那个时代的答案。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　七届二中全会针对党员干部作风的六条规定：一、不做寿；二、不送礼；三、少敬酒；四、少拍掌；五、不以人名作地名；六、不要把中国同志同马恩列斯平列。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“不做寿，这条做到了；不送礼，这个还有问题，所以反‘四风’要解决这个问题；少敬酒，现在公款吃喝得到遏制，关键是要坚持下去；少拍掌，我们也提倡；不以人名命名地名，这一条坚持下来了；第六条，我们党对此有清醒的认识……”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　是的，在作风问题上，党始终努力保持清醒。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　从七届二中全会六条规定到中央八项规定，始终绷紧的是作风这根弦，始终保持的是赶考的心态。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

	　　历史孕育着真理，往日的教训转为后世的鉴戒。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　2012年，在主持党的十八大报告起草工作时，习近平主张写下这样一段话：“发展中国特色社会主义是一项长期的艰巨的历史任务，必须准备进行具有许多新的历史特点的伟大斗争。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　新的国内外环境下，执政考验、改革开放考验、市场经济考验、外部环境考验……挑战不断；</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　新的发展阶段中，精神懈怠危险、能力不足危险、脱离群众危险、消极腐败危险……暗流涌动。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“杭州雷峰塔是怎么倒掉的”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在河北省委常委班子专题民主生活会上，谈到作风建设，习近平突然这样发问。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　接着，他作出分析：</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　“就是因为去捡砖的人多啊，今天你拿一块，明天他拿一块，最后塔就轰然倒掉了。倒下来是顷刻之间的事，但过程是渐进的。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　他语重心长地说：“有的事，总觉得不是燃眉之急的事，但恰恰是危亡之渐啊！”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　——面向未来的赶考，共产党人必须肩负起时代的重任。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　从党成立的那一天起，共产党人就踏上了赶考的旅程。为了答好这份考卷，多少先烈不惜抛头颅、洒热血；为了考出一个好成绩，多少共产党人殚精竭虑、鞠躬尽瘁！</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　时代已经给出新的考题。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　习近平说，中国共产党人能不能打仗，新中国的成立已经说明了；中国共产党人能不能搞建设搞发展，改革开放的推进也已经说明了；但是，能不能在日益复杂的国际国内环境下坚持住党的领导，坚持和发展中国特色社会主义，还需要一代一代中国共产党人继续作出回答。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　在西柏坡，习近平谈到当今时代的考题：“中国梦，两个百年，我们正在实现第一个百年的征程中，这是有时间表的，考试仍在继续。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　对于这场考试，他有着清醒的自觉：“我们新一届中央领导集体接过了党、国家、人民交给我们的沉甸甸的接力棒，我们一定要接好这一棒。”</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　——面向未来的赶考，共产党人必须引领民族的希望。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　作风在抓，风气在变。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　干部群众评价，党的十八大以来，以习近平同志为总书记的党中央，开新局、树新风，给中国带来新气象、新希望。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　第一批教育实践活动中，其他中央政治局常委深入联系点，实地调研，给予有力指导。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
	　　李克强指出，集中解决群众反映强烈的突出问题，使党全心全意为人民服务根本宗旨更好落实，使人民政府为人民性质更好体现。</p>
<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">

<p style="padding-bottom: 0px; widows: 2; text-transform: none; background-color: rgb(255,255,255); text-indent: 0px; margin: 15px 0px; padding-left: 0px; padding-right: 0px; font: 14px/23px 宋体; white-space: normal; orphans: 2; letter-spacing: normal; color: rgb(51,51,51); word-spacing: 0px; padding-top: 0px; -webkit-text-size-adjust: auto; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px">
