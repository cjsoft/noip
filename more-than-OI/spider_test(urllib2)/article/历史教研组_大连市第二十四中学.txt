<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); ">    </span><font color="#000000">The History Teaching and Research Group of Dalian No.24 high school consists of 5 professional teachers, among whom there is 1 postgraduate and 1 with the qualification of postgraduate programm. It’s a teaching and research group with high degrees, high titles, high quality and reasonable structure whose main body is middle-aged teachers. Teachers in this group was honored as “Liaoning Excellent History Educator Province”, “Liaoning Top Ten Young and Middle-aged History Educator” and “Core Teachers from schools under the Education Bureau”. They are also hired by Dalian Institute of Education as councilman of Senior High History Master Hand Workshop in Dalian and as members of College Entrance Examination Advising Center and by China National Institute for Educational Research as members of Teaching Materials Writing Group. Their lesson were selected as China National Institute for Educational Research’s curriculum criterion history national training demonstrating class. They have published many research papers on national publication &lt;History Education&gt;. They have been working at many education research topics and they have written nearly 20 teaching works.</font></p>
