<h4>
<font color="#000000">    在学校与各接待家庭的共同努力下，我校友好学校美国拉玛高中代表团回访接待工作落下帷幕。<br/>
	    2010年6月4日晚，拉玛高中代表团一行19人，包括教师2人，学生17人，到达大连，进行为期14天的友好访问。在此期间，学校为美方学生安排了体育课，劳技课，英语课，语文课，书法课，围棋课等课程，让美方学生深入中国课堂，全面感受中国高中生的学习和生活状态。学校各社团也积极组织活动，为中美双方学生提供多种平台，互展所长，让美方师生认识到了一个更趋国际化的二十四中，进一步加深了中美双方的交流与了解。<br/>
	    除此之外，各接待家庭也积极参与和配合。不但为美方学生安排了丰富多彩的旅游观光活动，还在生活上对他们关怀备至，提供无微不至的照顾。这既充分展现了中国人热情好客的一面，也让美国师生了解到了二十四中学生与家长的优秀。在进一步扩大中国的影响，帮助世界正确认识中国，传播中国的文化艺术、文化传统和价值观念等方面做出的贡献。</font></h4>

<h4>
<font color="#000000">My name is Charlotte Blakemore.  I live in Houston, Texas.  I am fifteen years old.  I go to Lamar High school and in August I will be in the tenth grade.  I chose to go to China because my parents suggested me to travel around the world and learn about different cultures and languages.  As well as making new friends across the world.  China is a very interesting place so I wanted to see it myself. </font></h4>
<h4>
<font color="#000000">I started my journey in April when I decided to go to China during my summer break.  I was very excited.  I was excited about meeting the people, the food, and getting to travel all around.  On Thursday, June 3, 2010 we left Houston early in the morning.  Even though I was tried I was nervous.  I was nervous about meeting my host family and making sure everyone arrived safely.  We traveled for over thirty hours so the Lamar students were tried but very excited to meet our host families. My host student is a kind girl named Claire.  She is 14 years old and a 10th grade student in Dalian No.24 middle school. My host dad and mom are all very caring and nice.<br/>
	We arrived on Friday and went home to go directly to sleep.  On Saturday I got to go to my very first BBQ in China.  I was excited to go to the beach but scared of what the food would taste like.  It turned out to be some of the best food I had ever tasted.  On Sunday, Claire and I went to New Mart it is bigger than the shopping center back in Houston.  I was surprised that it was so big.  Later we went to the supermarket.  It was very different than the ones in Houston.  This one was crowded and they sold a lot of different things.  I was afraid that I would lose Claire in the crowd of people. It was interesting to see all the food that they had, especially all the fruits and vegetables.  That night Claire and I went to her school for the astronomy club.  I got to look through a telescope and see Saturn and its rings.  I had never seen it before so I was very happy to get to see it in a city that is so big with bright lights.  Monday was my first day in school at Dalian No. 24 middle school.  I was worried that the people would look at me funny or be mean to me but they were welcoming and as kind as they could be.  The Lamar students went to an amusement park in the afternoon.  We were nervous that the rides would be scary but they turned out to be a lot of fun.  On Tuesday my class got to observe the entire school do tai ji.  It amazed us of how they all moved at the same time.  We later went to the labor park where we got to see ladies doing tai ji again.  We also got to see people singing and playing music.  It made me happy to see that all the people were outside on a beautiful day enjoying themselves and making the people around them happy also.  On Wednesday my class went to the Polar World.  We were excited to see all the shows and different fish.  The Polar World was bigger than the one in Houston and more interesting.  My favorite thing at the Polar World was the penguins because they are so cute and there were so many of them.  In the afternoon we went back to school and learned manual skills.  It was hard at first but then as I got what to do it became easier for me to do.  On Thursday, the Lamar students learned to play badminton and we went to English classes.  The classes were a lot of fun.  Later in Afternoon we went to the local TV station.  We got to observe them filming a show on astronomy.  It was interesting because we got to ask questions to the astronomer that came and he answered them very clearly. One of the Lamar students, Ian Dun, asked the question: do you think life exists on other planets?  The astronomer answered that he did think there were living things in our solar system but not as complex as humans or aliens.  Friday, the Lamar students go to go to classes with our host students.  I really enjoyed getting to see the way that the students learn here as apposed to the way we learn in America.  My favorite class was English because the class got to play games and the Lamar students got to teach our Chinese friends English.  There are some big differences between school at Lamar and school at Dalian 24.  At Lamar we only have four classes a day that last ninety minutes while at Dalian 24 they have nine classes a day and they last forty minutes.  At Lamar we spend about seven hours at school but at Dalian 24 the students spend at least twelve hours at school.  Lamar has a total of about thirty students in each classroom while Dalian 24 has about sixty per classroom.  At Dalian 24 they spend all day in one classroom and the teachers come to them but at Lamar we switch classrooms every period and the teachers stay in their classroom all day.  Friday afternoon the Lamar students had a party with our Chinese friends. We showed them modern dances in America and they showed us how the Chinese dance. Alex, one of the Lamar students, played the pi pa.  He taught himself how to do it.  Alex is a very hard working student and has taught himself Chinese also.  On Saturday the Lamar students got to travel to the country side.  We climbed a mountain and visited the sight of the battle between Japan and Russia.  I really enjoyed seeing all the beautiful scenery.  </font></h4>
<h4>
<font color="#000000">We have done many fun things this trip so far and I look forward to what it to come.   </font></h4>

<h4>
<font color="#000000">    我的名字叫夏洛特•布莱克梅，我家在美国德克萨斯州的休斯敦。我今年十五岁，在拉马高中读书，今年八月份我将是十年级的学生。我的父母希望我到世界的不同地方旅行，了解不同的文化和语言，同时结交世界各地的朋友。中国是一个非常有意思的地方，所以我想自己来看看。<br/>
	    当今年四月份我决定在暑期到中国时，我就开始准备我的旅行了。我非常的兴奋，我对于见不同的人，尝试不同的食物，四处旅行非常激动。2010年6月3日星期四早上，我们很早就从休斯敦出发了。我们渡过近30个小时的旅程后来到了大连，所有的拉玛高中学生都筋疲力尽，但见我们的接待家庭又使我们非常激动。我的接待学生是一个亲切的女孩，克莱尔（熊宠乔）。她十四岁，在大连市第二十四中学读高一。我的接待家庭父母都非常好，很关心我。<br/>
	    我们周五到大连后，就直接回家睡觉了。周六我去了来中国的第一次沙滩烧烤，我对于去海边非常兴奋，但是有些害怕食物会是什么样子的，结果是这是我吃过的最好的食物。星期天的时候，我和克莱尔去了新马特购物。新玛特比休斯敦的商场大很多，我对于它如此之大感到非常惊奇。然后我们去了超市，这里的超市和休斯顿的超市很不一样。这里非常拥挤，卖很多不同的东西，我很怕在人群中和克莱尔走散。看着所有的食物尤其是水果和蔬菜是一件非常有意思的事情。那天晚上我和克莱尔去了她学校参加24中天文社活动，我从一架望远镜里看了土星和它的光环。我以前从来没看过，所以我很高兴能透过明亮的灯光在这样的大城市里看星空。周一是我到24中的第一天，我很担心其他人会像看玩笑一样看我，或是对我不友善，但是他们都很欢迎我，尽他们所能的友好。<br/>
	    星期二的时候，我们有机会观看整个学校的课间操，学生们都在打太极。我们惊奇的是所有的学生都在同一时刻随着音乐转动，非常整齐。之后，我们去了劳动公园，在这里我们还看到很多老奶奶打太极，我们也看到了尽情享受音乐与歌唱的人们。让我感到快乐的是，在这么晴朗的日子里，大家都在户外享受生活，同时也让周围的人感到无比快乐。在周三，我们去了极地馆。我们非常兴奋地观看了所有的表演，还有那些各种各样的鱼类。这个极地馆比休斯敦的水族馆更加大，更加有趣。我最喜欢的极地动物是企鹅，因为它们是一群群的，特别可爱，在下午我们回到学校去上手工课，学习如何做手工艺品。刚开始做手工作品很难但是后来，随着我逐渐掌握技巧，制作变得越来越简单。在周四，拉玛高中的学生学习如何打羽毛球，然后和中国学生一起上英语课，这些课都很有趣。晚上的时候我们去了当地的电视台，去录制一个天文节目。有趣的是，我们有机会向天文学家提问题，他也能做出简明易懂的解释。一个拉玛高中的同学，伊恩•顿向天文学家提了一个问题“你相信地外生物吗？”天文学家回答他确信有地外生命的存在，但是他们没有人类这么高度进化。 周五，我们和我们的接待家庭的孩子一起上课，我们很高兴能得到机会了解这里学生的学习方法与我们美国的不同。我最喜欢的课程是英语，原因在上课有趣的游戏中，我们能教会周围中国朋友纯正的英语。大连市第24中学与我们拉玛高中有许多不同。在拉玛高中，我们一天有四节课，每一节课持续90分钟，而在24中，这里有九节课，每节课持续40分钟。在拉玛高中，每个班级大约有30个同学，而这里有六十个同学。在24中学生全部待在同一个教室，而在拉玛高中老师总是在一个教室，而学生每节课都要换教室。<br/>
	    周五下午，拉玛高中的学生和中国朋友们一起享受开聚会的乐趣。我们为他们展示美国的现代舞，他们也展示了中国舞蹈。一位我们拉玛高中的同学，埃里克森尝试打乒乓球。他是一个勤奋的学生，自己学习打乒乓球，他甚至还自学了中文。周六拉玛高中的学生去乡间旅游。我们攀爬了一座山，参观了日本和俄国的战争遗迹。我的确很喜欢参观这些美丽的风景。<br/>
	    我们在这里做了很多欢乐的事情，我也很期待再次来到大连！<br/>
