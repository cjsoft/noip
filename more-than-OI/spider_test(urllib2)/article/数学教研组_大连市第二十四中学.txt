<p align="left">
<font color="#000000">Mathematics Department</font><br/>
<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); ">    </span><font color="#000000">There are 18 excellent teachers in the Mathematics department of Dalian No.24 middle school. Among them, there are one special-grade teacher, 13 senior teachers, 2 key teachers of Dalian, 1 accomplished teacher  of China, 1 accomplished teacher  of Liaoning Province, 1 model worker of Dalian. Mathematics department is not only one of the Country Collaboration body members of the Mathematical Olympiad, but also an advanced group which has a thinking of high-quality, newly-updated education, high-level in their profession, so many comprehensive and outstanding teachers. In recent years, a number of teachers published dozens of papers in the journals of our country at all levels, and published dozens of monographs. In the provincial and municipal courseware production, high-quality lessons, and talking about the class competitions, many teachers won the first prize. Lots of teachers give their lessons to the audience as an example in mathematics teaching and research activities. Groups of teachers have access to the title of provincial, city, school "Excellent Party Member." Besides these, students guided by them had many  achievements in the  College Entrance Examination in mathematics in the province, some of them also won the first prize in the National Mathematics Competition. As a leading department in our school, Mathematics department was given the title of excellent “ Advanced Collective” in Zhong Shan District and in Dalian.</font></p>
