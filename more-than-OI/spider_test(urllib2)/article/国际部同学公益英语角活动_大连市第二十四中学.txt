            24中英语角活动，是为励志考入24中的初中生提供练习口语的机会，一般情况下1对6-7，由24中学国际部高一同学独立承办。参加活动的孩子为小学五年级至初三的学生，由24梦想群家长组织。<br/>
      当天参加活动的高一国际部同学有：张厚博、黄天祎、王曦晔、林镇瀛、慕翔宇、张博一、刘熙宸、刘熙源、王彦鹏、董姝君、吉蔚晗、谷育萱、吕莘雨、洪博洋、陈娴蓉、吕佩柔<br/>
      当天英语角的话题如下：<br/>
First English Corner Topics in October<br/>
□ Interests and Hobbies<br/>
1。What’s your hobbies? Are your hobbies mostly intellectual or athletic or something else? What do you like about those specific hobbies?<br/>
2. If you have ten minutes of free time, how do you like to use it? One hour? One day? One week? If you unexpectedly have a couple hours of free time, what kinds of spontaneous things do you like to do for fun?<br/>
3 If you had to read a book about something not related to your homework, what would you like to read about? If it were a nonfiction book, what topic would you choose? Why you like it? If it were a fiction book, what genre of fiction would you read?<br/>
4 Do you think that internet and tv have killed hobbies in China? Do you have any friends who don’t like to use the internet or watch tv? If you couldn’t use a computer or tv for a year, how do you think you would spend your time differently?<br/>
5 which TV program you like most?   Why ?   Give brief description<br/>
 <br/>
□ Travel （Optional , in case you still have time with your team ）<br/>
1. Do you like travelling? Why? Why not?<br/>
2. Which places have you visited in China? Which is your favorite destination/ city? Why?<br/>
3. Which one do you prefer when you travel?<br/>
(1) Package tour/DIY tour<br/>
(2) Long trip/short trip<br/>
(3) Travel in peak season/off-peak season<br/>
(4) Travel alone/with friends or family<br/>
4. Have you ever been abroad? Which country do you like to visit? Why?<br/>
5 If you have enough time and money, what ‘s your travel plan ?
