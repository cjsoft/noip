<span style="color: rgb(0,0,0)">    </span><font color="#000000">地理组是一个富有活力、朝气蓬勃的教研组，由五名中青年教师组成。地理组规模虽小，人员虽少，但皆以“组兴我荣，组衰我耻”为己念。组内老师相互合作，相互促进，群策群力。</font><br/>
<span style="color: rgb(0,0,0)">    </span><font color="#000000">07年我组荣获大连市巾帼文明岗。我组教师中，有徐连红、战新英两名高级教师，彭喜治、刘驰罡两名中级教师。地理组的全体成员皆曾获得过省级以上优质课、论文、教学设计、课件制作等一等奖，并有多篇文章见诸报刊、杂志。地理组“以研促教”的教研组经验在东北三省年会上交流，引起专家、同行的重视并被广而告之，在大连市校际间也有着很大的影响力。</font></p>
<p align="left">
<font color="#000000">Geography Department</font><br/>
<span style="color: rgb(0,0,0)">    </span><font color="#000000">Geography Department is lively and energetic, consisting of only five young teachers. However, they always bear the principle of “Take pride in the department’s prosperity” in mind. The teachers cooperate with each other, promote each other and always have concerted efforts.</font><br/>
<span style="color: rgb(0,0,0)">    </span><font color="#000000">In 2007 the department won “Jin-guo Civilization” title. Of the teachers, Xu lianhong and Zhan Xinying are high-ranking teachers while Peng xizhi and Liu Chigang are intermediate teachers. All the teachers have won the first prizes in Class Presentation, Papers, Teaching Designing and Courseware Making. And many articles have been published in newspapers and magazines. They exchange their experience of “Promoting Teaching through Research” in the Northeastern Annual Meeting and the experts and other teachers presented attached great importance to it. It had a great influence on many high schools in Dalian.</font></p>
